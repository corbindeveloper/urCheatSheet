Text Tags

h1-h6 are what's known as header text elements. These elements decrement in size with h1 being the largest and h6 being the smallest.

Sometimes you need to bold or italicize certain text or maybe just a word. You can utilize these texts even inside another text element such as an h3.

Citation and address are two more specific tags and have a more specific use case.
Cite creates a citation that is usually processed in italics. On the other hand, address creates an address section usually process in italics.
